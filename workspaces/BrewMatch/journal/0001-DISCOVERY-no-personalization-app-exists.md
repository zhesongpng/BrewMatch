# DISCOVERY: No Coffee App Offers Bean-Specific Personalization with Taste Learning

Date: 2026-05-09
Phase: /analyze
Source: Competitive landscape research (01-research/01-competitive-landscape.md)

## Finding

The competitive analysis revealed that **no current coffee brewing app offers bean-specific recipe personalization with a taste learning loop**. Every existing app operates in one of two modes:

1. **Static recipe libraries with timers** (Filtru, Brew Timer, Cofi) — same recipes regardless of beans or taste history
2. **Brew logging journals** (Beanconqueror) — records what happened, does not suggest what to change

This gap is genuine and unoccupied. Beanconqueror is the most feature-rich competitor (free, open-source, 30+ parameters, smart scale integration) but explicitly has no personalization or ML.

## Significance

This validates BrewMatch's core differentiation. The gap between "logging what you did" and "suggesting what you should do next, specific to YOUR beans and YOUR taste" is real and no competitor fills it.

However, the gap exists because willingness to pay for this capability is unproven. For the course project, the gap is an advantage — novel application domain for ML demonstration.

## How to Apply

- Lead the course presentation with "no existing app does this" as the motivation
- The gap IS the ML demonstration opportunity — not a product opportunity
- Reference specific competitors (Beanconqueror, Filtru) to show due diligence
