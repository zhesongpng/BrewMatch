# RISK — B1.5 dialed-in dose is not persisted until B1.6

## What

B1.5 added an editable "Your Dose" field that rescales the on-screen brewing
guidance (water + every pour) to the dose the user actually weighed. It is
display-only by design: the brew record saved on submit still uses the recipe's
original dose, not the dialed-in value.

## Why it matters

A user who carefully sets 18 g, brews, and checks history will see the recipe's
original 15 g — the dose they actually used was never recorded. Until this is
closed, brew history and the personalization engine receive the _assumed_ dose,
slightly degrading data quality, and the "coffee left in the bag" countdown has
nothing real to subtract.

## Bound / disposition

This is the explicit B1.5 → B1.6 split, not a B1.5 defect. B1.6 (next active
todo) wires the scaled recipe + `actual_dose_g` + bag link into the brew record
and the running-low count. The `_scale_recipe` helper B1.6 consumes already
exists and is tested. Logged here so the gap is visible if B1.6 slips.
